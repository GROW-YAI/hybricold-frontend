Why Choose Us

Smart Cooling: Transforming Agricultural Harvest Potential

Hybricold Limited is a pioneering agri-tech company dedicated to combating post-harvest losses in Ghana by providing hybrid renewable energy-powered cold storage solutions for smallholder tomato farmers, leveraging innovative technology to enhance food security and agricultural sustainability.

  • By extending tomato shelf life from 5 to 21 days, we directly increase farmers' income and reduce food waste, creating economic opportunities in rural communities.
  • Our renewable energy-powered cold storage systems address critical agricultural challenges, supporting food security, reducing economic vulnerability, and promoting sustainable farming practices across Africa.
Our Services

Empowering Farmers Through Innovative Cold Storage Solutions

Mobile Cold Storage

Portable cold storage units designed to provide immediate post-harvest cooling for smallholder farmers, reducing spoilage and extending produce marketability in remote agricultural regions.

Energy Optimization

Hybrid renewable energy integration services that customize cold storage infrastructure, enabling farmers to reduce operational costs and minimize carbon footprint while maintaining optimal produce preservation.

Technical Training

Comprehensive agricultural technology training programs that educate farmers on cold storage management, proper produce handling techniques, and sustainable preservation strategies to maximize economic potential.
